Description

Take your instant noodles to the next level with this Quick Homemade Ramen. In just about 20 minutes, you'll build a savory broth infused with garlic, ginger, and dried shiitake mushrooms, then simmer ramen noodles directly in it. Bright kale, shredded carrots, and scallions add freshness and texture. Top it off with crunchy panko crumbs, a soft boiled egg (optional), chili oil or sriracha, and sesame oil, and you've got a cozy, flavorful bowl ready to nourish and delight. Perfect for busy weeknights when you crave comfort with a clean, fresh twist.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Broth (chicken or veg)
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Veggies like carrots or kale
  • All your favorite toppings like some panko, egg, chili oil, etc.

Steps

  1. Stir-Fry The Aromatics - Garlic and ginger, what a delicious duo. This is where the flavor is, friends.
  2. Make Your Broth - Add some chicken broth and dried shiitake mushrooms for some umami punch.
  3. Add Noodles - Cook your noodles right in the broth with some scallions (more flavor, please!).
  4. Add Veg - Thinly sliced kale, shredded carrots, whatever you'd like! Cook until just tender.
